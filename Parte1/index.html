Tribute to Steve Jobs

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."

TIMELINE

  • Feb 1955 Steven Paul was born in San Francisco, the son of Abdulfattah Jandali and Joanne Schieble. He is quickly adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • Aug 1997 Steve Jobs introduces Apple's new board of directors and a truce with Microsoft at Macworld Boston.
  • 12 Jun 2005 Steve Jobs makes a memorable commencement speech at Stanford University.
  • 27 Jan 2010 After months of wild rumors, Steve Jobs unveils iPad, 'the biggest thing Apple's ever done'. The tablet runs the same operating system as iPhone.
